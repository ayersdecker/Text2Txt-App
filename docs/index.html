About Text 2 Txt OCR Mobile App

Text 2 Txt OCR Mobile App is a mobile application that uses optical character recognition (OCR) technology to convert images of text into editable text files. With Text 2 Txt OCR Mobile App, you can easily scan documents, receipts, and other types of text-based images and convert them into digital text files that you can edit, share, and store.

Features

  • High-quality OCR technology
  • Ability to recognize multiple languages
  • Fast and accurate scanning
  • Easy sharing and storage options
